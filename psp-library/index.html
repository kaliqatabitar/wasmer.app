FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining PSP Library.

Can I change my shift after joining?
+
Yes — you can switch shifts at the start of next month by speaking to library staff directly.
Is library open on Sundays and public holidays?
+
Yes! PSP is open every single day — Sundays and public holidays included — 7 AM to 10 PM.
Is there storage for bags and belongings?
+
Yes, secure storage is available inside the library for all members at no extra cost.
How do I pay the monthly membership fee?
+
Fees can be paid in cash or via UPI at the library counter at the start of each month.
Which exams do students prepare for here?
+
REET, RAS, RPSC 2nd Grade, SSC, Railway, Patwari, Police and all other competitive exams.
