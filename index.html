Fueling Ambitions Since 2020

Established on January 5, 2020, by Amar Kamboj, Perfect Solution Point Reading Library was born out of a simple need: a quiet place to think. In the bustling heart of Sri Karanpur, Rajasthan, we have built a sanctuary for serious students who believe that focused preparation is the only key to success.

Over the past 5 years, we have hosted over 1,000 students who have gone on to achieve their dreams. Our facility is not just a room with chairs; it's a productivity hub equipped with ergonomic furniture, noise-cancellation aesthetics, and high-speed fiber connectivity to bridge the gap between hard work and smart results.
